--- title: "Admissions" ---

    Prerequistes:

  • An Lower Seconds Class degree or better (or equivalent) in Computer Science, Statistics, or Mathematics

    OR

  • An Upper Seconds Class degree in another suitably numerate discipline subject to taking qualifying courses
  • Previous experience in Data Science and/or Statistical Analysis would be an asset
Given the interdisciplinary nature of Data Science as well as the rigour of the degree's constituent courses, it is vital that students be well versed in programming, basic proability and statistics, and linear algebra. To this end, we recommend that students consider reinforcing their command of these areas using the following resources:
  • Linear Algebra - Linear Algebra - Foundations to Frontiers
  • Probability and Statistics - Introduction to Probability - The Science of Uncertainty
  • Programming in Python - Introduction to Computer Science and Programming Using Python
